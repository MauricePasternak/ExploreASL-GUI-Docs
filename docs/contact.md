## For ExploreASL-GUI related questions

You can contact the maintainer and developer of ExploreASL-GUI, [Maurice Pasternak](mailto:maurice.pasternak@utoronto.ca).

You can contact the general [ExploreASL email address](mailto:ExploreASL.LAB@gmail.com) with your inquiries and the appropriate person will be dispatched to answer your question.
